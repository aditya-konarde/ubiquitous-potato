🚫 YouNope

Block YouTube channels forever. Never see their videos again.

How it works

  1. Click the extension icon on any YouTube page
  2. Enter a channel's @handle and hit Block
  3. Their videos vanish from your feed, search, and recommendations
  4. If you land on their page — you get a cat instead 🐱

Import blocklist

Got a list of channels to block? Import a .txt or .csv file with comma-separated channel names. The @ prefix is optional.
